Microsoft 365 Healthcare FAQs

Answers to common questions about healthcare-grade Microsoft 365 deployments.

Is Microsoft 365 compliant for healthcare in Australia?

Yes, when configured correctly. We implement DLP, retention, and security controls to meet ADHA and Privacy Act requirements.

Can we use Teams for patient video consultations?

Yes. We configure virtual waiting rooms, meeting locks, and secure collaboration policies.

Which Microsoft 365 license tier is best for clinics?

We typically recommend Business Premium for smaller practices or E3/E5 for larger organisations.

How do you handle data migration?

We use Microsoft migration tooling with secure staging and low downtime. Email history and documents are preserved.
