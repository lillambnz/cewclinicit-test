Layered Network Architecture for Healthcare

Five layers of protection built to isolate clinical data and meet compliance requirements.

  • Layer 1: Perimeter Security Firewalls, DDoS protection, and intrusion prevention.
  • Layer 2: Network Segmentation Isolated clinical, administrative, and guest networks.
  • Layer 3: Access Control MFA, conditional access policies, and role-based permissions.
  • Layer 4: Data Protection AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.
  • Layer 5: Redundancy & Failover NBN plus satellite backup with automatic switchover.
