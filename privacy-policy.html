We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Complete contact forms or consultation requests

This information may include:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Practice or organization information
  • Job title and professional role
  • Practice size and demographics
  • Current technology systems and challenges

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

2.3 Third-Party Information

We may collect information from:

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under Australian Privacy Law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Complain: Lodge complaints about privacy breaches

How to Exercise Your Rights

7. Data Security and Retention

7.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: SSL/HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
  • Staff Training: Regular privacy and security training

7.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Active Clients: Duration of engagement plus 7 years
  • Prospects: 3 years from last interaction
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe or opt-out
  • Legal Requirements: As required by Australian law

7.3 Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected individuals within 72 hours where required
  • Report to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)
  • Take immediate steps to secure and remedy the breach
  • Provide ongoing updates and support

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for healthcare professionals and business users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Obtain new consent where required
