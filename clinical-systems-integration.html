Real Australian Clinic Scenarios We Solve

Every day challenges faced by GP clinics across Australia - and how we fix them

Scenario: Mixed Billing Complexity

Perth Metro Clinic

The Problem:

A busy Perth clinic offers bulk billing, private billing, and workers compensation services. Staff manually calculate fees, apply Medicare rebates, and process different payment methods across 3 separate systems. Patient billing takes 20 minutes per consultation.

Our Integration Solution:

  • Automatic fee calculation based on patient type and service
  • Real-time Medicare eligibility checking
  • Integrated EFTPOS and health fund claiming
  • Workers comp billing directly to insurers
  • Automated statements and receipts

Result:

Billing time reduced from 20 minutes to 2 minutes per patient - saving 3 hours daily and eliminating billing errors.

Scenario: Pathology Result Delays

Regional WA Practice

The Problem:

Critical pathology results arrive via fax, email, and online portals. Staff manually check multiple sources, print results, and file them in patient records. Urgent results get missed or delayed, creating patient safety risks.

Our Integration Solution:

  • Direct integration with PathWest, Clinipath, and other labs
  • Automatic result importing into patient records
  • Alert system for abnormal or urgent results
  • Automated patient notification workflows
  • Digital result sharing with specialists

Result:

Zero missed critical results and 90% faster result processing with automatic patient follow-up.

Scenario: Medicare Claiming Inefficiencies

Multi-Doctor Practice

The Problem:

Each doctor uses different item numbers and claiming procedures. Medicare claims are processed manually at end of day, creating cash flow delays. Rejected claims pile up and revenue is lost through forgotten resubmissions.

Our Integration Solution:

  • Real-time Medicare claiming during consultation
  • Automatic item number validation and optimization
  • Immediate payment confirmation or rejection alerts
  • Automated resubmission of corrected claims
  • Comprehensive claiming analytics and reporting

Result:

98% first-time claim acceptance rate and immediate payment processing improving cash flow by $15,000 monthly.

Scenario: Specialist Referral Chaos

Suburban Family Practice

The Problem:

Specialist referrals are created manually, faxed or posted, and tracking is done via phone calls. Patients lose referrals, specialists can't access patient history, and follow-up care is fragmented.

Our Integration Solution:

  • Digital referral creation and transmission
  • Integration with specialist booking systems
  • Automatic patient history and test result sharing
  • Real-time appointment confirmation and updates
  • Automated specialist report receipt and filing

Result:

100% digital referrals with automatic tracking, reducing lost referrals to zero and improving specialist communication.

Scenario: Rural Connectivity Challenges

Remote WA Clinic

The Problem:

Unreliable internet affects cloud-based systems. Staff work offline, creating sync issues when connection returns. Emergency telehealth consultations fail due to system incompatibilities.

Our Integration Solution:

  • Hybrid cloud-local system architecture
  • Automatic offline mode with intelligent sync
  • Backup connectivity via mobile networks
  • Optimized data compression for slow connections
  • Emergency mode for critical patient care

Result:

99.9% system availability even with poor internet, ensuring continuous patient care and data integrity.
